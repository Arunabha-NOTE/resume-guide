Experience

Experience is often the most important section of your resume for recruiters.

Each experience entry should include company name, position title, dates, location, and detailed bullet points describing your contributions.

Example Format:

ABC Startup
SDE Intern
June 2025 – Present
Remote
– Contributing across multiple cross-functional projects spanning cloud infrastructure, full-stack web development, mobile app development, generative AI, etc.
XYZ Company
DevOps Intern
May 2025 – July 2025
Remote
– Working with Azure cloud services and CI/CD pipelines to streamline development workflows.

Do's and Don'ts

Do's

  • Use action verbs and quantify your achievements with specific metrics when possible (Don't Make shit up).
  • Be clear and concise with your points.

Don'ts

  • Don't just list job responsibilities; focus on your accomplishments and impact.
  • Don't use "AI" and "ATS" websites to add AI generated buzz words and fake metrics like 15% more efficient without sufficient data to back it up.
  • Don't include fake paid internships and don't fake it till you make it. Sometimes you need to produce a certificate. Also fake internships have no credibility (Not to mention a scam).
