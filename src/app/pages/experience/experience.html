Experience

This section will show what your work experience should look like. Experience is often the most important section of your resume for recruiters.

Each experience entry should include company name, position title, dates, location, and detailed bullet points describing your achievements.

Example Format:

Heizen
SDE Intern
June 2025 – Present
Remote
– Contributing across multiple cross-functional projects spanning cloud infrastructure, full-stack web development, mobile app development, generative AI, etc.
Celebal Technologies
DevOps Intern
May 2025 – July 2025
Remote
– Working with Azure cloud services and CI/CD pipelines to streamline development workflows.

Do's and Don'ts

Do's

  • Use action verbs and quantify your achievements with specific metrics when possible.

Don'ts

  • Don't just list job responsibilities; focus on your accomplishments and impact.
