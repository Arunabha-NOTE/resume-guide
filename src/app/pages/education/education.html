Education

The education section of your resume showcases your academic background and qualifications.

Your education section should include your degree(s), institution(s), graduation date(s), and relevant information.

Since we are pursuing a college degree, we can omit school details (optional).

Example Format:

Manipal University Jaipur Expected Graduation: July 2026
B.Tech. Computer and Communication Engineering Jaipur, Rajasthan

Do's and Don'ts

Do's

  • List education in reverse chronological order (most recent first).

Don'ts

  • Try and avoid adding your cgpa. Even if you have 9+. If you want to show your impressive cgpa you can but i have seen that a lot of times 9 Cgpa people seem to be compensating for a lack of skills and projects. So consider this before adding your cgpa.
