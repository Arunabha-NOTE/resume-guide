Education

This section will show what it should look like. The education section of your resume showcases your academic background and qualifications.

Your education section should include your degree(s), institution(s), graduation date(s), and relevant achievements.

Example Format:

Manipal University Jaipur Expected Graduation: July 2026
B.Tech. Computer and Communication Engineering (Minor in Cloud Computing) Jaipur, Rajasthan

Do's and Don'ts

Do's

  • List education in reverse chronological order (most recent first).

Don'ts

  • Don't include high school if you have a college degree.
