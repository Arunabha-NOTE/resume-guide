Achievements

This section will show what your achievements section should look like. Achievements highlight your accomplishments, awards, and recognition.

Include awards, honors, competitions, publications, or any significant recognition that demonstrates your excellence and impact.

Example Format:

Best Innovation Award
2024
– Received recognition for developing an AI-powered solution that improved customer service efficiency by 45%.
– Selected from over 200 submissions across the organization.
Dean's List
2023-2024
– Achieved academic excellence with GPA above 3.7 for consecutive semesters.
– Recognized among top 10% of students in Computer Science department.

Do's and Don'ts

Do's

  • Quantify your achievements with specific metrics and impact when possible.

Don'ts

  • Don't include participation awards or achievements from high school if you're a college graduate.
