Header

This section will show what the header of your resume should look like. The header is the first thing recruiters see, so it needs to be clear, professional, and contain all essential contact information.

Your header should include your full name, location, and contact information displayed in a clean, organized format.

You can include your linkedin, github, leetcode whatever you think you want to showcase the most along with your contact details.

* Please do not add a Summary after the header of yourself. Might as well write the cover letter seperately.

Example Format:

John Doe
Jaipur, Rajasthan, India
johndoe@example.com
linkedin.com/in/johndoe
github.com/johndoe
www.portfolio.com

Do's and Don'ts

Do's

  • Use a clear, professional font and ensure your name stands out prominently.
  • Customize the links according to what you think is adding to the cv.

Don'ts

  • Don't include a link to github or leetcode if they are empty (How about trying to fill it first?)
  • Avoid a second line of links you want the space for other sections.
