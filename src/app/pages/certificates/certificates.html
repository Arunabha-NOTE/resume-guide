Certificates (Optional)

Certificates demonstrate your commitment to continuous learning and professional development.

Include professional certifications and relevant training that adds value to your profile.

If you don't have such certificates just skip this section. You can add your coursera, nptel to Linkedin. Only add them if you feel your resume is empty or its one of those professional coursera courses (IBM DevOps for example).

Example Format:

AWS Certified Solutions Architect
January 2024
Google Cloud Professional Developer
January 2023

Do's and Don'ts

Do's

  • Include certificates that are relevant to your target role and industry.
  • Try to add professional certifications from recognized institutions only (e.g., AWS Certified Cloud Practitioner).

Don'ts

  • Don't include outdated certifications or those with expired credentials.
  • Don't add coursera, udemy or nptel certificates those are more or less useless. Use these platforms for learning instead.
  • Don't add certificates of completion or attendance either. Just don't.
