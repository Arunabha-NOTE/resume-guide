Certificates

This section will show what your certificates section should look like. Certificates demonstrate your commitment to continuous learning and professional development.

Include professional certifications, online course completions, and relevant training that adds value to your profile.

Example Format:

AWS Certified Solutions Architect
2024
– Amazon Web Services certification demonstrating cloud architecture expertise.
– Covers designing distributed systems, security, and cost optimization on AWS platform.
Google Cloud Professional Developer
2023
– Professional certification for developing scalable applications on Google Cloud Platform.
– Demonstrates proficiency in cloud-native development and DevOps practices.

Do's and Don'ts

Do's

  • Include certificates that are relevant to your target role and industry.

Don'ts

  • Don't include outdated certifications or those with expired credentials.
