Leadership/Extracurricular

This section will show what your leadership and extracurricular activities should look like. These experiences demonstrate your soft skills, leadership abilities, and community involvement.

Include positions of responsibility, volunteer work, clubs, organizations, and activities that showcase your character and additional skills.

Example Format:

Student Government Association
Vice President
Sep 2023 – May 2024
– Led a team of 15 student representatives to advocate for student needs and implement campus improvements.
– Organized and managed events for over 2,000 students, increasing participation by 35%.
Computer Science Club
Technical Lead
Jan 2023 – Present
– Mentored 20+ junior students in programming and guided them through technical projects.
– Coordinated weekly coding workshops and hackathons, improving club engagement by 50%.

Do's and Don'ts

Do's

  • Focus on leadership roles and activities that demonstrate relevant skills for your target position.

Don'ts

  • Don't include every activity; be selective and focus on meaningful experiences.
